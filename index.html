About Pairs of the Day

Pairs of the day is a classic memory card game played with a set of cards. Each card comes with a front image and back image. The player selects two cards and if the card images match, then they stay facing up. However, If the two cards donâ€™t match then they go back to a face down position. The whole process continues until all pairs have been found.

How to Play

  1. Choose your difficulty by selecting the game mode. Easy, Normal or Hard.
  2. Once selected click the start game button.
  3. Click on a game tile to reveal a club logo.
  4. Then click on a second game tile to reveal another club logo.
  5. If both club logos match they stay face up otherwise they go back face down.
  6. You repeat this process untill you match all pairs for that difficulty.
